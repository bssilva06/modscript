## 3. What the App does

The App provides three modes:
- **Generate:** translates plain-English requests into AutoModerator YAML.
- **Explain:** translates existing AutoModerator YAML into plain English.
- **Conflict Check:** flags structural patterns in your config (duplicates, redundant rules, suspicious ordering) for your review.

All AI output is generated by Google's Gemini API. The App does not validate generated YAML against AutoModerator's actual runtime behavior. **The Conflict Check mode is structural pattern analysis, not runtime simulation, and does not predict which of your rules will fire on any given post or comment.**
